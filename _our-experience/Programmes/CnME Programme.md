<p>Background of CnME</p>
<p>Established in 2012, CnME (pronounced as see-and-me) is a holistic life-skills
development&nbsp;programme for all Secondary One to Secondary Three Nanhuarians.
The ‘C’ refers to our school mission: Confident, Compassionate and Culturally
Versatile. The ‘n’ refers to our school niche areas in Environment Education,
Performing Arts and SAP. The ‘ME’ refers to the Nanhuarian developing their
competencies in MOE 21st Century Competencies, Leadership and ICT skills.</p>

<p><strong>CnME: A Fortified Lifestyle 2023</strong>
</p>
<p>CnME starts with Oral Communications programmes in Secondary One and culminates
in the Secondary Three CnME Modules. CnME aims to inculcate essential life
skills in Nanhuarians through the context of our school niche areas to
develop their confidence, cultural appreciation and creativity.</p>
